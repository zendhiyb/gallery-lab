Laboratory of Game and Multimedia

The Game and Multimedia Laboratory at the Informatics Assistant Laboratory (Aslab Informatika) of UMSIDA provides the latest hardware and software to support learning and research in the fields of game and multimedia. This facility helps students develop practical skills through projects, workshops, and training sessions.

Laboratory of Computer Network

The Computer Systems and Data Communication Laboratory provides facilities for practicals, research, simulations, and discussions in data communication and computer networks. It supports both hands-on activities and theoretical learning, helping students enhance their skills through projects, workshops, and training programs.

Laboratory of Algorithm and Programming

The Algorithm and Programming Laboratory at Aslab Informatika UMSIDA provides up-to-date hardware and software to support learning and research in algorithms and programming. This facility helps students build practical skills through software development, problem-solving, and various projects and training focused on programming techniques and algorithm design.

Laboratory of Computing Lab

The Computer Laboratory at Aslab Informatika UMSIDA provides the latest hardware and software to support learning and research in computer technology. It helps students develop practical skills through the use of computers for software development, data analysis, and various projects and training related to information and computer technology.

Laboratory of Intelligent Systems

The Intelligent Systems Laboratory at Aslab Informatika UMSIDA provides the latest hardware and software to support learning and research in artificial intelligence and intelligent systems. This facility helps students develop practical skills through AI algorithm development, machine learning, and projects and training focused on smart technology applications across various fields.

Laboratory of Software Engineering

The Software Engineering Laboratory at Aslab Informatika UMSIDA provides the latest hardware and software to support learning and research in software development. This facility helps students build practical skills through software design, development, testing, and training focused on software engineering techniques and lifecycle management.

Laboratory of Research and Reading

The Reading and Research Room at Aslab Informatika UMSIDA provides computers, desks, and floor seating to support learning and research. This space offers a comfortable environment for students to work individually or in groups, and to explore innovative ideas with access to the latest hardware and software.
